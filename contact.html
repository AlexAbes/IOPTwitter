Who We Are

We are a group of Harvard students brought together by Harvard's Institute of Politics as part of its STEAM initiative for science and technology in politics. We all have some kind of computer science background, but we are all equally passionate about politics and data.


Members:

Alex Abrahams, Molly Cinnamon, Matthew Leifer, Monica Mishra, and Samuel Plank.

Supervised (and occasionally scolded) by Professor Jim Waldo.


Get in touch:

We're new to this, so if you have suggestions about what we could do next with this data or an idea about how we could better visualize it, please let us know by contacting our project chair, Alex Abrahams, at abrahams[at]college[dot]harvard[dot]edu.
